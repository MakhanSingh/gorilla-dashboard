STEP 2

Connect your seller accounts

1. Go to your Seller Accounts in the left menu.

2. Click on the green add account icon in the top right corner and add the seller accounts.

3. Copy the link and provide it to the Seller Account owner to authorize the connection.

4. Select the marketplace and click the authorize button.

5. Seller Central will open up. Authorize the connection from Seller Central.

6. The seller account owner will see a success page and an email will be sent to the Gorilla ROI user.

7. Wait for the connection to finish and you will be redirected to your Gorilla account and the connection will show up as active.

8. Add a nickname to easily identify the seller and to use in the formula.

9. Add emails of your Account Users.

10. Wait for the data to import. Check status on your account under download status.

STEP 3

Install and activate the addon

1. Start in incognito.

2. Log in to your main Google Sheets (Workspace Account highly recommended).

3. Install the addon for Single Business Plan and Free Versions Download link.

4. Once installed go to Google Sheets-> Menu > Add-ons > "Multi-Seller (Agency)" -> Use this addon.

5. Menu -> Addons -> "Multi-Seller (Agency)" -> Add User ID.

6. Enter your Agency ID you can find from your Gorilla Account dashboard.

7. If it doesn't save, redo in incognito mode because Google is caching your G Drive.

8. Wait for the data import process. Will take a couple of hours or more based on your size.
